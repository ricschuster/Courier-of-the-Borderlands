Art

The map, markers, and panel frames are skinned with Kenney's asset packs. All three are released under Creative Commons Zero, which asks for nothing in return. They are credited here anyway, because the work deserves saying out loud.

Kenney makes these packs public domain so anyone can build with them. If they are useful to you too, they are at kenney.nl.
