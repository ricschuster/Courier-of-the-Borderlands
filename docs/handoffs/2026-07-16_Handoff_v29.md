## Summary

Ran the first deep automated persona playtest: 10 players (8 scripted policy
personas, a live exploit hunter, a blind screenshot-only player) played the
real 0.4.0 build with wear ON through the e2e bridge, orchestrated as a
background workflow. Full report and draft issues are in
`docs/playtests/2026-07-16_persona_playtest.md` and its companion draft-issues

## Completed

- Persona harness built (untracked `tmp-personas/`): parameterized driver
  forked from autoplay.mjs with policy hooks, chunked runs resuming through a
  persistent browser profile, per-event JSONL telemetry, and an aggregation
  script. Building it surfaced that autoplay.mjs still uses raw one-shot key
  presses and silently drops inputs (trap 4's tapKey face).
- All 8 scripted personas completed the three-region arc independently; no
  crashes, soft locks, or console errors anywhere.
- Confirmed exploit (high): page reload is a free tow home; the save does not
  persist courier position. Repro: `tmp-personas/exploit/repro-reload-free-tow.mjs`.
  Found independently by two personas, re-confirmed after the run.
- Confirmed bug (code level): board digit input is not gated on the
  summary/capstone panels the way board visibility is (same class as #292).
- Caught our own harness artifact: 6 concurrent browsers roughly doubled wear
  per delivery via frame starvation. Solo control runs established the real
  economy: diligent player pays a 56% repair tax, banks 889c, never strands;
  the no-spend miser finishes at a 100% tax, 10 strandings, and 2.2x the time.
- The owner's previously closed exploits all verified still closed, with
  measured margins (limp tax 6.3x, tow never economical, atomic encounter
  saves, integer ledger, #292 guard holds, e2e switches inert in production).
- Blind-player onboarding audit: first delivery fully discoverable from the
  screen; the second contract is a cliff (fog-hidden pass, flipping compass,
  untaught off-road wear) ending in a stranded-and-broke dead end.
- Overlay input surface characterized for #300; #298 finding: Off-road rank
  2-3 grants the access Marsh Treads / Salt Runners sell, and no organic run
  affords all three relief upgrades.
