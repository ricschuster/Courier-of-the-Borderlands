### Refactor: shrink map-scene.ts (#80, #81, #82)

  previously had none. Mirrors the existing `MapHud` / `objective.ts` patterns.

### Session 5 playtest fixes (#83, #84, #85)

- **#83** Toasts hold until the player presses Space (no fade timer, which was
  always too short or too long). A "Space: dismiss message" cue shows only while
  a toast is up.
- **#84** "Region Cleared" panel now fires on every region. It was gated on
  in-play contracts, and each spoke's arc-gated contract is revealed-but-
  undelivered as the mission climax, which suppressed the panel; now based on the
  standing (ungated) routes via `baseContractCounts`. New e2e regression.
- **#85** Warm return greetings at Tidewatch and Mossgate once their region is
  reconnected, instead of the cold first-meeting line (mirrors the postmaster
  fix; `textVariant` on each greeting).

### M5.4 "world reacts" investment (#86, #87, #88)

The owner chose "the world reacts" (over coin sinks or deeper skills) as the fix
for the late-game flatline. Three slices shipped against
`docs/design/06_world_state_remainder.md`:

- **#86** Reconnection reward premium: a delivery to an already-reconnected place
  pays +20% (`reconnectionRewardMultiplier` in `world-state.ts`), shown on the
  board and in the delivery note. World-state is read before the contract is
  marked complete, so the reconnecting delivery pays flat and only later work to
  a revived place is boosted.
- **#87** End-of-arc capstone panel: breaking the blockade at Greywater shows a
  ceremonial finale (journey in numbers, closing lines), once, the session it
  happens. Shown exactly once with no new save field via `blockadeBrokenAtLoad`.
  While up, it clears the toast, retires the summary, and hides the board.
- **#88** New work opens as a region reconnects: a reconnected place emits a
  derived `reconnected_<id>` flag (`reconnectedFlag`), and two second-wave
  lateral (spoke-to-spoke) routes per region gate on it, so the board refreshes
  as a region revives. New `Contract.arc` marker keeps the Hidden Road journal
  thread counting only the arc contracts, not this ordinary gated work.
