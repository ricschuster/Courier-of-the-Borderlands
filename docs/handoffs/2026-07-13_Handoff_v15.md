## Current state

What works:
- `main` clean and green; everything above shipped.
- **Both tuning values human-validated 2026-07-13:** the owner did a quick
  playthrough and confirmed the #185 road pace (1.2x) and the #186 Fenmarch 2.2x
  wear "all good." These are now settled defaults, not provisional.

Key finding: Fenmarch's softness was a curve-inversion problem, not geography. By
region 3 a big tank plus maxed relief/Off-road soak up wear, so more marsh would
not have bitten. `wearMultiplier` targets exactly that. Measure at 2.2x: Fenmarch
went from the easiest region to the highest-wearing one (wear ~140 -> ~237, min
condition 79 -> 58, the run ends near-broke). It is the proven lever if another
region ever reads too soft.

Measurement caveat carried forward: local `travel-sink-measure` / autoplay runs
strand more than a careful human (both ran ~6.2m = 2x clean on this machine, so
the greedy driver over-drives and inflates wear). Trust the human read over the
local strand counts.
