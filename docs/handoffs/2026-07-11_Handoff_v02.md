## Completed

### CI fix: capability-gate soft-lock (#102)

The on-merge / nightly `Arc` job had been red since #100, failing
deterministically with the courier stuck at ~(25,19) short of Reedgrave. Root
cause: impassable colliders are baked once at scene `create()`. `unlockFeature`
removed them for fords, but a capability gained mid-scene (buying an upgrade or
ranking a skill) fired no unlock event, so the deep-mire's collider went stale:
pathfinding routed through the now-passable tile while physics still blocked it.
It "passed locally" only when the driver happened to trigger a scene restart
(which rebuilds colliders) at the right moment.

- Fix: a generic `refreshGatedColliders()` (map-scene) called after any upgrade
  purchase or skill rank, destroying colliders for every capability token now
  held. This was also a real player bug (buy Marsh Treads in Greybridge, mire
  stays invisibly blocked until you leave and re-enter).
- Guard: `tests/e2e/mire-route.spec.ts` ranks Off-road to 2 in-scene and drives
  across the mire; verified it fails without the fix.
- This fix is a prerequisite for any new capability gate, so it had to land
  first.

### Roads-gate slice 5: spread gates to both spokes (#103, #104)

Owner design calls (this session): both spokes get a gate, using a NEW
capability rather than reusing `mire-crossing`, and each gate is a
Reedgrave-style premium corner locale (new settlement behind a barrier, long way
always open).

- New mechanic: `tidal-flat` terrain + `tidal-crossing` capability, granted by
  the new **Salt Runners** upgrade (140 coins) OR **Off-road rank 3**. Escalates
  from the mire (Off-road 2 -> tidal 3); Salt Runners is the coin path and a
  fresh sink. One mechanic, reused in both wetland regions.
- **Saltreach (#103): Saltmere** at (19,9), behind a salt lagoon down column 18,
  tidal crossing at (18,9). Premium standing contract
  `saltreach-cipher-to-saltmere` (118 coins). Crossing saves ~6 tiles from
  Tidewatch, ~10 from Saltkeep.
- **Fenmarch (#104): Fenholt** at (17,7), behind a fen mere walling the pocket
  north (row 6) and south (row 8) across columns 16-18, tidal crossing at
  (16,7). Premium standing contract `fenmarch-cipher-for-fenholt` (115 coins).
  Crossing saves ~4 tiles from Mossgate.

Level-design method (important for the next spoke-style content): these maps had
no natural gate-able detour (verified with the pathfinder: a channel crossing
saved zero tiles), so each gate is a deliberately-carved pocket. Every design
was validated against `findPath` before committing: base-reachable the long way
(so the arc never soft-locks) and strictly shorter with the key. The throwaway
probe pattern (a temp `tests/unit/_probe.test.ts` that renders the map and
prints distances) is the way to design these; delete it before shipping.
